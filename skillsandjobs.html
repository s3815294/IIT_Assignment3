UX Designer


Our team is looking for someone that can help us make our recycle app enjoyable, usable and accessible. We could make use of someone that can bridge the gap between users, development team and business stakeholders. Therefore, employing UX Designers in our team would be perfect.

Responsibilities

-Able to conduct User research
-Prototyping and user testing
-Be able to take care of images, colour schemes, icons, and typography in the app
-Perform Information Architecture however we like.


Skills needed

-Flexible and Adaptable when working it a team
-Good Verbal Communication and Organisational Skills
-Knowledge in Business
-Know how to do prototyping and user testing
-Basic Front End Coding


Mobile Developer


Our team is looking for a mobile developer that can help us develop an app for our project. They are responsible for creating these software programs in accordance with the proper mobile platforms. Since our project is mostly going to be used in mobile phones this position would be great to employ. This position involves making applications for Android, Ios and other platforms focused on mobile devices.

Responsibilities

-Work out our team’s needs and converting them into technical requirements.
-Develop application programming interfaces (APIs).
-Cooperate with function designers, UX designers, UI designers and clients.
-Ensure the correct linking of the backend and frontend.
-Updated in terms of terminology, concepts and do good practices for coding mobile apps.
-Working closely with colleagues to constantly innovate app functionality and design.

Essential Requirements

-Be able to develop mobile apps for both ios and android.
-Be able to ensure correct linking of frontend and backend.
-Know the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
-Knowledge of Java web applications.
-Good communication and organisational skills


Data Scientist


Our team, The FAB Five, is looking for a data scientist to help bring our project to the next level. In this role, you will help in extracting meaning from data and interpret the data itself.. Since our project involves a database, someone who can spend a lot of effort collecting, cleaning, and munging it would be nice to employ. Persistence, statistics, and software engineering abilities are required for this procedure, as are skills for understanding data biases and debugging logging output from code. Someone who has natural curiosity and is creative and critical thinking.

Responsibilities

-Identifying the data-analytics issues that provide the most potential for the company.
-Choosing the appropriate data sets and variables.
-Bringing together enormous amounts of organised as well as unorganised data from various sources.
-Data cleaning and validation to assure accuracy, completeness, and consistency.
-Creating and using models and algorithms to mine large data sets.
-Data analysis to find patterns and trends.
-Data interpretation to find solutions and opportunities

Essential Requirements

-Background in computer programming. Examples include Python and R.
-Experience in building Machine learning models.
-Good verbal communication and organisational skills.
-Requires knowledge of maths or statistics.


IT Sales


Lastly, our team needs someone that can find us the right technological hardware and software products to suit their business needs. Therefore, an IT sales person would be the last puzzle piece to our perfect team.

Skills needed

-Good presentation skills and a professional telephone manner.
-Time-management skills.
-Good verbal communication and organisational skills.
-Business Knowledge.
-Updated technical skills for new technology.
